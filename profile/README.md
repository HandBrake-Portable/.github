# HandBrake Portable – Complete Video Transcoding on the Go

A powerful, portable **HandBrake Portable** solution designed to simplify video conversion without installation. This **HandBrake Portable** tool provides a full-featured interface for encoding, compressing, and converting media files, allowing users to carry their entire video workflow on a USB drive.

## About HandBrake Portable
**HandBrake Portable** is a professional-grade video transcoder built specifically for users who need flexibility and portability.  
**HandBrake Portable** gives you access to advanced encoding engines (x264, x265, NVENC, AMF, QSV), enabling high-quality conversions, batch processing, subtitle embedding, chapter marking, and audio mixing.  
**HandBrake Portable** offers desktop-level functionality while running entirely from a portable folder without touching the system registry. Whether you are a content creator, media archivist, or IT professional, **HandBrake Portable** ensures you always have reliable transcoding at your fingertips.  
**HandBrake Portable** works on any compatible computer instantly, preserving all your presets and settings across sessions.

2. Launch `HandBrakePortable.exe` directly from the folder (no installation required).  
3. Load your source video, DVD, or Blu-ray folder.
